In this 5 star example, we used the Meteo vocabulary to represent the temperature forecasts. Each forecast has a URI (for example, 'forecast for Monday, 15 November 2010'), which can be used in another context. Additionally, through linking the place (#Galway) to the DBpedia entry Galway, we have disambiguated it. Eventually we provided context for the interpretation of the #temp through the respective typed links to DBpedia (Temperature and Celsius).
